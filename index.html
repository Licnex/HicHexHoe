Game Rules

  • The board is an effectively infinite hex grid.
  • X starts and places exactly 1 piece on the first turn.
  • After that, players alternate turns placing exactly 2 pieces.
  • Pieces can only be placed on empty hexes.
  • First player to make 6 in a row in any hex direction (besides hex diagonal) wins instantly.
